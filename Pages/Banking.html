Banking

AI in banking is growing faster than we thought. Many of the banks have used this technology for prevent bank frauds. HDFC bank is best example of this. HDFC Bank has developed an AI-based chatbot called EVA (Electronic Virtual Assistant), built by Bengaluru-based Sense forth AI Research. EVA has also addressed many of the customers queries within 0.4 seconds. So, use of this technology is providing security for Account holders.
