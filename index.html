Artificial Intelligence and its Applications

It is claimed that Artificial intelligence will take the place of human capability in many areas. In other words, if people can teach the computer like how a young child does then it is called as an Artificial intelligence. It can also apply their knowledge and represent itself in the world like a human. So, we can say that it will work like a human and beyond that. So, Artificial intelligence is the simply simulation of humans which is done by programmers. By programmers’ point of view, Artificial intelligence is the study and developments of intelligent machines and software that can reason, learn, gather knowledge, communicate, manipulate and perceive the objects.

It has advantages over the natural intelligence as it is permanent, consistent and fast. If we apply this technology in robotics then we can create a human which has multi-ability and this robot can do anything we want. Let’s look at one example if human wants to become a doctor or engineer then it will take around 4-5-year time. But if we have this technology then we will have to just download some books and save it in data base. So, it will take too small time. So, this technology is faster as compare to human.

Marvel movies and other Hollywood movies have also supported our claims. JARVIS which is the program of iron man is best example of Artificial intelligence

There are many areas of working when this technology is very useful. AI technology offering real practical benefits in many of their application.

AI has many areas which is described below:

Expert Systems, Natural Language Processing, Speech Understanding, Robotics and Sensory Systems, Computer Vision and Scene Recognition, Intelligent Computer-Aided Instruction, Neural Computing. From these Expert System is a rapidly growing technology which is having a huge impact on various fields of life. The various techniques applied in artificial intelligence are Neural Network, Fuzzy Logic, Evolutionary Computing, and Hybrid Artificial Intelligence.

In the article, our main focus on Artificial intelligence application which includes 8 domains. we also include what happens when machine take complete role of human and what is the advantages of this technology.
